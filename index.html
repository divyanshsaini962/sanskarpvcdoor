WHAT IS A PVC DOOR

A PVC door, also known as a uPVC door, is a type of door made from polyvinyl chloride (PVC) material. PVC is a durable and low-maintenance plastic that is commonly used in construction and building applications.

PVC doors have gained popularity in residential and commercial buildings due to their various advantages. They are known for their excellent insulation properties, which help in maintaining temperature inside the building and reducing energy consumption. PVC doors are also resistant to weather elements, including sunlight, rain, and wind, making them suitable for outdoor use.

In terms of security, PVC doors can be equipped with multi-point locking systems and reinforced panels to enhance safety and deter break-ins.
